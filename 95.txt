ABOUT OCTOPUS
Octopus is a fast-growing company with more than 600 employees. Since we launched in 2000, we’ve established market leading positions across financial services, healthcare and energy.
The Octopus Group incorporates Octopus Energy, Octopus Healthcare, Octopus Investments, Octopus Property, Octopus Ventures and Octopus Labs. Octopus Labs is the fintech innovation unit of the Octopus Group. We fuse track record and technology, expertise and UX, to build financial products the way they should be: trustworthy, rewarding and easy-to-use.
ABOUT THE ROLE
The Head of Data Architecture will help define the vision, strategy and principles for data management and understand how data is, or will be used and the implication on people, processes, products and technology. You will be expected to understand the current legacy systems through analysis and plan for future development and growth.
The position is an opportunity for a highly motivated person to work in a business-critical, high-visibility role for an innovative fast growing financial services company.
Essential Job Functions
· Lead in the data technology planning and execution
· Define data technology strategy
· Conduct current state assessment of our Data Warehouse and data processes
· Define the future state data architecture
· Responsible for ensuring that project data requirements are met
· Lead and support a team of Senior BI developers
YOU:
Experience taking a lead role in architecture, designing and building data warehousing systems
Technically proficient, especially SQL Server 2008 onwards and having experience working with Azure SQL DBs, Cloud SQL and BigQuery
High level of knowledge of T-SQL scripting & debugging (stored procedures, user functions etc.) and SSIS package development.
Extensive experience as a database and ETL(Extract, Transform, Load) developer for data ingestion and manipulation and maintaining ETL pipelines
Experience with orchestration, monitoring and alerting in ETL pipelines like Airflow, Zaloni or similar
Experience working in Agile development environments
Experience in Python, Hadoop, and Spark
Technical Leadership experience including code and technical design reviews and mentoring junior engineers
Other Job Specific Experience/Technical Skills/Abilities
Degree educated preferably in a Technology discipline
Business Intelligence and Enterprise Architecture Design
Financial Services Investment Management industry experience highly preferred
Experience in helping organisation's define their Data Technology Strategy
A deep understanding of architecture discipline, processes, concepts and best practices
Hands-on experience defining enterprise-level data architectures and data modelling (including conceptual, logical and physical)
Good understanding of the inter-relationship between the data architecture and the enterprise architecture stack (business, application, infrastructure)
Detailed experience of systems integration, data warehousing and data mapping projects
Excellent verbal and written communications skills
Excellent inter-personal skills, with experience in working with senior management
Proven self-working and motivational skills
Ability to see the bigger picture whilst still be comfortable with the detail

About OctopusLabs
Octopus is a fast-growing company with more than 500 employees. Since we launched in 2000, we’ve established market leading positions across financial services, healthcare and energy.
The Octopus Group incorporates Octopus Energy, Octopus Healthcare, Octopus Investments, Octopus Property, Octopus Ventures and Octopus Labs.
Octopus Labs is the fintech innovation unit of the Octopus Group. We fuse track record and technology, expertise and UX, to build financial products the way they should be: trustworthy, rewarding and easy-to-use.
We’re not your typical financial services company. We want to change the world of finance for the better, with easy-to-use products and services that help people to manage their money. We combine established financial expertise with cutting-edge technology to build smart, simple and disruptive fintech solutions for the mass market – like Octopus Choice and Octopus Cash.
18 months ago, we launched our first product, a peer-to-peer lending platform called Octopus Choice. We now have over 5,000 customers who have together invested more than £110 million, and are growing at a rapid pace.
Working at Octopus Labs provides an opportunity to combine the exciting pace and confidence of a start-up, with the broader benefits of a large, well-capitalised business that manages more than £7 billion on behalf of over 100,000 customers.
