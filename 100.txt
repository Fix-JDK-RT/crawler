Job description
The Western Mutual Insurance Group has been providing excellent customer service to homeowners throughout the Southwestern United States for over 75 years. We are rated A+ (Superior) by A.M. Best Company and have been named among the Top 50 Property Casualty Insurers in the country by Ward’s.
We are looking for a knowledgeable, detail oriented experienced Senior Software Architect to join our team. The successful candidate will be a fast learner with the ability to handle multiple tasks and possess excellent analytical, listening and communication skills, both oral and verbal; and will be able to work independently as well as collaboratively. This position is for a hands-on developer who can work in an agile environment as a team member helping to complete sprints. We need someone who can create readable and well maintainable code that is thoroughly unit tested.
Some of the job duties include: researching, designing and writing new code; maintaining existing code; testing, troubleshooting and debugging new and changed code; responsible for projects from design to deployment.
We offer a competitive salary depending on experience and a comprehensive benefits package. 
Please submit your resume for consideration.            NON SMOKING OFFICE
Job Requirements
Education – Bachelor’s degree
Programming Experience – 7+ Years with Full stack Microsoft.NET Core, C#,
Proficiency in Object Oriented design and programming
Professional experience with SQL/NoSQL (MongoDB).
Experience with Micro Service Architecture is highly preferable.
The preferred applicant will possess the following skill set:
Experience in the Insurance, Finance, or E-commerce industries
Languages: C#, SQL, Object Oriented knowledge,
Technologies: WebAPI, Entity Framework, NoSQL, REST, Docker, RabbitMQ
Methodologies: Relational Database Design, Unit & Integration Testing, Object Oriented Design
Optional skills
JavaScript/ ReactJS/PHP
Entity Framework
