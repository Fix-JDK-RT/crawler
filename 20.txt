Develop Your Future
We believe that diverse experience contributes to a broader collective perspective that will consistently lead to better products. Every person we hire makes Stack Overflow an even better place to work.
View Openings
Our Benefits
Health & Wellness
Full health coverage for you and your family (Stack Overflow pays 100% of your premiums), and a $150 monthly fitness reimbursement. You’ll also have free gourmet lunches everyday if you work in one of our offices.
Work Where You Want
We have a remote-first culture. Remote employees get all the equipment we have in our offices, including an ergonomic chair and desk. Teams hold annual in-person meetups, and standard meetings are video chats by default so no one gets left out.
Parental Leave
We offer paid maternity and paternity leave, a $500 meal delivery stipend, and the primary caregiver has the option to come back to work part time for up to 1 year.
Learning & Development
We actively encourage continued education through a conference allowance, tuition reimbursement and external training. We value a team that’s always striving to be better.
