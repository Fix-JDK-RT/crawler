Job description
Are you looking for your next challenge?  Are you a solid Java developer, with frontend development and DevOps experience? Do you have experience in Solution Design and/or Requirements Engineering?  Would you like to work in a team where you are trusted and empowered to deliver high-quality, customer-centric software?  As part of this Agile team, you will bring your knowledge and skills to all phases of the software engineering process.  You will work within a multidisciplinary environment where passion, trust, open communication, and innovation are highly valued.
Main duties
Join team working on Proof-of-Concept based on a Semantic Web language to support completion of PoC, and assist in design and implementation of end-solution based on PoC outcomes
Design and development of full-stack solutions, based on a Java backend and React frontend
Strong collaboration with the product owner and other team members in order to provide robust requirements and specifications
Ship high-quality, thoroughly reviewed code on time; support software in production
Actively participate in regular Scrum ceremonies
On-call responsibilities 
Your profile
Degree in Computer Science or a similar field
5-7 years web application development experience (Java, Spring, JavaScript ES6, HTML/CSS, npm, node.js, webpack, Oracle SQL)
1-2 years Solution Design or IT Requirements engineering experience
DevOps knowledge (scripting, Jenkins, Docker, SaltStack, OpenShift)
Familiar with Agile software development methodologies
Strong work-ethic, able to work independently, able to prioritize tasks
Communicative team-player
Excellent verbal and written skills in German and English
Your Benefits
SIX develop: Personal development plan and career opportunities
SIX on the move: We takes over a part of the costs if you commute with the public transport
SIX balances: Annual working time, min. 5 weeks of holidays, mortgages %, in-house fitness
SIX leisure: Versatile and attractive offer for joint sports and leisure activities
SIX team: A diverse, hard-working, fun group of individuals
We are looking forward to receiving your complete application electronically.Your future started now, apply to SIX!
